# Article Reader

**Route:** `/knowledge/[slug]`
**Owner:** Member 1

## Page content

- Article title, summary, author/editor, published date, updated date, topic, and reading time.
- Reading progress and desktop table of contents.
- Accessible article body with figures, captions, pull quotes, and data callouts.
- Inline citations plus full references/sources section.
- Share controls and related articles.
- Not-found state for unknown slugs.

## Acceptance

- Heading hierarchy and anchor links are valid.
- Citations connect clearly to references and external links are identified.
- Long-form content is readable on small screens.
