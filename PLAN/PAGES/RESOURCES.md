# The Reading Room

**Route:** `/resources`
**Owner:** Member 3

## Page content

- Intro explaining who the library is for.
- Search plus filters for topic, audience, type, and year.
- Resource cards with title, summary, publisher, format, date, file/link, and size where applicable.
- Featured downloads/toolkits and clear external-link treatment.
- Empty, loading, and unavailable-file states.

## Acceptance

- Every download/link works and has meaningful accessible text.
- Filters are combinable and resettable.
- Resource metadata is consistent and driven by the resource data model.
