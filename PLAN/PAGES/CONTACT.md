# Contact

**Route:** `/contact`
**Owner:** Member 3

## Page content

- Short contact introduction and response-time expectation.
- Contact details and Hyderabad location information.
- Inquiry form with name, email, subject/category, organisation, and message.
- Links to Get Involved and Resources for common requests.
- Success confirmation plus recoverable error state.

## Acceptance

- Required fields, email format, length limits, and server errors are handled.
- Contact information is approved and clickable where appropriate.
- The form is keyboard accessible and does not clear data after a failed submission.
