# Get Involved

**Route:** `/get-involved`
**Owner:** Member 3

## Page content

- Clear ways to participate: contribute knowledge, partner, volunteer, suggest a topic, or share educational material.
- Expectations and eligibility for each participation type.
- Involvement form with role, interest, organisation, contact details, and message.
- Downloadable/shareable toolkit if approved assets exist.
- Partner area only for confirmed partners.

## Acceptance

- Form validates clearly, provides success/error feedback, and prevents duplicate submission.
- Privacy/consent language appears before submission.
- No fake partner logos or inactive download links.
