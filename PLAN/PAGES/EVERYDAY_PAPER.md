# Everyday Paper

**Route:** `/everyday-paper`
**Owner:** Member 2

## Page content

- Hero showing how paper appears in daily life.
- Filterable grid of products across hygiene, food, medicine, education, packaging, construction, and technology.
- Product cards explaining the paper component, function, recyclability/handling, and an interesting fact.
- Responsible-use and disposal guidance without overgeneralising local recycling access.
- Links to Glossary, Circularity, and Games.

## Acceptance

- Filters work on mobile and include a clear-all action.
- Product claims have sources where they are not common knowledge.
- Images have useful alt text and cards remain understandable without them.
